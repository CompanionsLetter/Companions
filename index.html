How to Bible study in an effective way?

Well, mostly all ways are effective, but the one that I think makes you understand and remember the most is this one:

    U will need:

  • A Book Bible
  • A phone or laptop
  • And a notebook (optional)

The Book Bible will be the main thing; you will start reading in it. After you read some verses from the Book Bible, switch to your phone or laptop for the Bible in the English version.

(BTW, read 4-8 verses in the book, then switch to the English Bible and read the same 4-8, and continue just like that until you finish the chapter.)

If you find any important verse, write it in the notebook. If you understand a chapter and want to write how you understand it, write it in the notebook. AFTER all of this Book Bible and English Bible, you will need to go to YouTube and search for the chapter you have just read.

I recommend watching the YouTube channel: Bible Project. They explain it in a unique way; for example, they take a whole book—Galatians—and Galatians has 6 chapters. In the video, they draw and explain stuff, dividing it into 3 sections: Chapter 1-2 / Chapter 3-4 / Chapter 5-6.

So you can do all of the readings and note-taking process above for only 1-2 chapters and then watch the video only for those chapters. Bible study: 2 chapters is enough for a day.

This is what I mean by sections:

If you dont have a Book Bible, just use the Bible app, but read it in your language and then in English.

This is how i think Bible studying is effective, if you have another way of doing it, then test which one is more effective.
